About Me

Career Goal

I plan to at least help someone in the world in some way or another. It doesn't have to be life-saving or state-of-the-art never seen before technology. I want to make people's lives better or easier, whether it be easier communication, game development, or simpler management. My plans for achieving this goal is to finish high school, complete my Bachelor's or Master's Computer Science degree, and settle down at a company where I can make decent money, while also working on side projects, if I have time available. I don't have a craving for money, but I'd like to at least have a nice house, and not eat Top Ramen every day.
